Privacy
Protocol.

01. Data Encryption

Endless Group utilizes high-level encryption for all user authentication. Your learning progress and mastery levels are stored within a secure Firebase infrastructure, ensuring that your cognitive data remains isolated and protected.

02. Identity Protection

We do not sell your personal information. Authentication is handled exclusively through Google OAuth to minimize the amount of sensitive data stored on our local servers. Only your public display name and email are used for account management.

03. Learning Analytics

We collect anonymized data on phrase interaction to improve our adaptive mastery algorithms. This data is used solely to refine the linguistic difficulty and category distribution within the Lexicon Pro vault.

04. Your Rights

Users maintain full sovereignty over their data. You may request account termination and data erasure at any time by contacting @code_essence via Telegram.
